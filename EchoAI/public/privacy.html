Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

EchoAI ("we", "us") is an AI-powered marketing platform operated by Blacketer Ventures. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to the EchoAI web application and any connected services you authorize.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your name, email address, password (stored hashed), and business details you provide during setup.
  • Connected service data — if you connect accounts such as Facebook, Google, Stripe, or Twilio, we receive the data those services share under the permissions you approve (for example ad account performance, page details, or campaign metrics). Access tokens are encrypted at rest.
  • Customer and lead data — information you or your website visitors submit through EchoAI tools such as the chat widget, lead forms, or CRM features.
  • Usage information — log data such as pages viewed and actions taken, used to operate and improve the service.

How we use information

  • To provide the EchoAI service: running campaigns, generating content, qualifying leads, sending reports and notifications you request.
  • To operate integrations you have connected, strictly within the permissions you granted.
  • To bill for the service (payments are processed by Stripe; we never store full card numbers).
  • To secure, maintain, and improve the platform.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your Facebook or Google data for anything other than the features you use inside EchoAI.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except the service providers needed to run the platform (hosting, payments, AI processing, email/SMS delivery) or when required by law.

Data from Facebook and Google

When you connect a Facebook or Google account, EchoAI accesses only the data needed for the features you use (such as managing ad campaigns or reading performance metrics). You can disconnect these accounts at any time from your EchoAI settings, which removes our access. You can also revoke access from your Facebook or Google security settings.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see our data deletion instructions. Once processed, your data is removed from our systems except where retention is required by law (for example billing records).

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and sensitive credentials (such as connected-account tokens) are encrypted at rest. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard practices.

Children

EchoAI is a business tool and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.
