How to remove your data from EchoAI

Delete your connected Facebook data

  1. Sign in to EchoAI and open Settings.
  2. Under connected accounts, choose Disconnect next to Facebook. This removes EchoAI's access and deletes the stored connection tokens.
  3. Optionally, in Facebook go to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Business Integrations, find EchoAI, and remove it there as well.

Delete your entire EchoAI account and data

Email us at jamesbbethanyb@yahoo.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" from the email address on your account. We will delete your account, brand data, leads, and connected-service tokens, and confirm by reply once complete. Requests are processed within 30 days. Records we are legally required to keep (such as billing history) are retained only as long as the law requires.
