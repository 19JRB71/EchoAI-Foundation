Here's Prompt 15: Public Landing Page and Signup Flow for EchoAI:
EchoAI is an AI-powered SaaS marketing platform. You've built the complete platform including the customer dashboard, admin panel, voice chatbot, email notifications, and everything in between. Now you need to build the public facing landing page that sells EchoAI to potential customers and captures signups before their call with you.
In the client source folder create a standalone public landing page that is completely separate from the authenticated dashboard. This is the page people see when they visit your website before they have an account.
The landing page should have six sections.
Section one is the hero. Bold headline that speaks directly to the pain of small business owners who are wasting money on Facebook ads that don't work. Subheadline that explains EchoAI in one simple sentence. A clear call to action button that says Book Your Free Demo and scrolls to the signup form. Use strong direct language in the Alex Hormozi style — speak to outcomes not features.
Section two is the problem. Three pain points small business owners feel. Wasting money on ads that don't convert. No time to manage campaigns and follow up with leads. No idea what their competitors are doing. Make it feel like you are reading their mind.
Section three is the solution. Explain how EchoAI solves each pain point. Automated campaigns that run and optimize themselves. AI voice chatbot that qualifies leads instantly. Competitor intelligence that shows you exactly what is working in your market. Keep it simple and visual with icons or simple graphics.
Section four is social proof placeholder. Three testimonial card placeholders with fake names and businesses that you will replace with real testimonials once you have customers. Include a results placeholder showing numbers like two hundred leads generated, forty percent lower cost per lead, and ten hours saved per week.
Section five is pricing. Show the three tiers clearly. Starter at fifty dollars per month for solo operators. Professional at fifty dollars per seat for teams up to five. Enterprise at fifty dollars per seat for unlimited users. Include a what is in each plan breakdown. Add a note that all plans include a free onboarding call with you personally.
Section six is the demo request form. Simple form asking for their name, business type, phone number, and email. When they submit it creates a lead record in the EchoAI database tagged as a platform inquiry and sends you an admin notification email. Show a thank you message after submission telling them you will call them within twenty four hours.
Make the landing page visually bold and modern. Dark background with bright accent colors. Mobile responsive. Fast loading. No clutter. This page needs to convert visitors into booked calls.
Create a separate public route in the Express backend for the demo request form submission that requires no authentication.
Update the React router so the root URL shows the landing page and the dashboard is at slash dashboard. Output all files created.
