Zorecho Core behavior

The Core must feel alive rather than appearing as a static image.

Idle
Soft breathing illumination
Gentle movement in the center waveform
Subtle outer-ring pulse
Slow ambient particles
Demo begins
Core brightens smoothly
Echo becomes active
Waveform begins moving
Agent connections illuminate as their departments are mentioned
Echo speaking
Center waveform reacts to the spoken audio
Outer rings pulse naturally
Cyan illumination increases
Small light pulses travel along the relevant agent connection lines
Echo’s agent node glows teal
The Core returns smoothly to idle after Echo finishes

No flashing, aggressive spinning, or distracting effects. The animation should feel premium, calm, and purposeful.

Echo introduction and demo

Do not autoplay audible speech when the page first loads.

Initially show the Core in idle mode with a clear button:

“Hello. I’m Echo, your personal AI assistant.
Good morning. Here is your example business briefing.

Seven customer emails need attention.
Voice answered twelve calls and booked three appointments.
Scout found two new competitor campaigns.
Nova prepared today’s social content.
Your AI company is online and ready. Would you like to see Mission Control?”
