# Market Intelligence Engine (MIE)

## Status

**Future Roadmap Feature**

Do **not** interrupt or delay current development. Add this to the long-term Zorecho architecture plan for future implementation.

---

# Mission

Build the most comprehensive AI-powered Market Intelligence Engine available for small and medium-sized businesses.

This system should continuously gather, analyze, compare, and interpret publicly available marketing intelligence from multiple trusted sources to help business owners make better marketing, advertising, SEO, branding, and expansion decisions.

This is **not** simply a keyword research tool.

It is Zorecho's central intelligence platform that feeds Scout, Sage, Atlas, Forge, Nova, Vision, and Echo with actionable business intelligence.

The objective is to answer:

* Where should I market?
* What are customers searching for?
* Where is demand growing?
* Which competitors are winning?
* Why are they winning?
* What should we do differently?
* What opportunities are we missing?
* Where should we expand next?

Every recommendation must be supported by evidence whenever possible.

# Safeguards

Never fabricate data.

Clearly distinguish:

Verified

Estimated

Predicted

User-supplied

Always display confidence scores.

Respect API licensing.

Respect search engine terms of service.

Respect Meta policies.

Never scrape protected or private information.

Never copy competitor creative.

Always preserve source attribution internally.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Market Intelligence Engine should become the strategic brain of Zorecho.

A business owner should simply be able to ask:

"Echo, where should I spend my next marketing dollar?"

or

"Where should I expand next?"

or

"What are my competitors doing better than me?"

Scout gathers the evidence.

Sage analyzes the data.

Atlas estimates advertising performance.

Forge prepares the creative strategy.

Nova prepares the content strategy.

Vision evaluates visual opportunities.

Echo delivers one clear, evidence-based executive recommendation backed by multiple public intelligence sources.

The goal is to make Zorecho the most intelligent marketing and business strategy platform available—not by relying on one search engine or one data source, but by continuously synthesizing insights from many legitimate public sources into actionable business decisions.
