Here's the complete Prompt 68: Sage — The Industry Intelligence Agent for EchoAI:
EchoAI is an AI-powered SaaS marketing platform with nine AI team members. Now you need to add Sage as the ninth and final member — the Industry Intelligence Agent. This is the answer to the question asked several prompts ago about how Sage should source its intelligence — the answer is Option A, real live web search. Please upgrade the Anthropic SDK to the latest version that supports the built in web search tool, run the full test suite after upgrading to confirm nothing breaks across any existing feature, then build Sage completely as specified below.
SAGE'S IDENTITY
Sage is the ninth AI team member. Title is Industry Intelligence Agent. Avatar color is deep emerald green. Description is "Studying your industry around the clock so your team always has the smartest possible strategy." Add Sage to the AI team sidebar, Mission Control team status grid, and build Sage's complete department view. Sage's status is always Active because Sage never stops working.
HOW SAGE SOURCES INTELLIGENCE
Sage uses real live web search through the Anthropic built in web search tool to continuously gather fresh intelligence from the internet. Sage does not rely on the AI model's training data which has a cutoff date. Sage reads actual live web pages, news articles, industry publications, research reports, Facebook posts, and social media content right now in real time.
Sage also searches Facebook public posts, Facebook groups relevant to each brand's industry, and the Facebook Ad Library to gather consumer sentiment, trending topics, competitor ad strategies, and community discussions. This social intelligence feeds directly into Nova's content strategy and Atlas's ad creative direction.
Sage runs on two schedules. Every 6 hours Sage runs a full deep research cycle reading fresh articles, industry news, competitor announcements, consumer sentiment, and market research for every active brand. Every 30 minutes Sage runs a lightweight urgent signal monitoring cycle checking only for breaking news, viral trends, major competitor announcements, and significant market shifts. When something urgent is detected Sage alerts Echo immediately without waiting for the next 6 hour cycle.
SAGE'S COMPETITOR ONBOARDING
During Sage's initial setup for each new brand Sage has a conversational onboarding where it asks the owner to identify their top competitors. Sage asks for competitor business names, website URLs, and Facebook page URLs. Sage then searches the web and Facebook to confirm it found the correct businesses, shows the owner what it found including follower counts and recent activity, and asks for confirmation before beginning monitoring.
If Sage discovers additional competitors through its ongoing research it brings them to the owner's attention immediately and asks whether to add them to the watch list. Sage never assumes it knows who the competitors are without owner confirmation. Sage maintains a confirmed competitor list for each brand that the owner can update at any time from Sage's department.
WHAT SAGE STUDIES
For every brand Sage builds and continuously updates a comprehensive industry intelligence profile covering industry landscape and market trends, consumer psychology including what motivates buyers and what language resonates, marketing best practices including what headlines hooks and offers are converting right now, seasonal patterns and upcoming demand opportunities, regulatory environment including what ad claims are allowed and what disclosures are required, competitive intelligence across the entire industry not just direct competitors, and emerging opportunities including new platforms new technologies and new consumer behaviors.
HOW SAGE FEEDS THE OTHER AGENTS
Sage shares intelligence through a shared knowledge base all agents access before acting. Atlas checks Sage's latest marketing best practices before building any campaign. Nova gets Sage's trending topic intelligence and content timing recommendations. Pulse gets Sage's consumer psychology intelligence for smarter follow-ups. Forge gets Sage's creative direction based on what visuals and messaging are resonating. Scout gets Sage's competitive landscape context to deepen its competitor monitoring. Echo incorporates Sage's intelligence into every morning briefing and every strategic recommendation referencing specific things Sage learned.
SAGE INTELLIGENCE INPUT BOX
Build a Sage Intelligence Input section in Sage's department with a drag and drop box where the owner can submit anything directly to Sage. Links get fetched and analyzed immediately. Facebook page URLs get added to the continuous monitoring list. Images and photos get analyzed using vision capabilities to extract marketing intelligence such as reading competitor ad creative, analyzing landing page design, or studying event photos. Documents and PDF files get read and incorporated into the industry knowledge base. The owner can also paste raw text directly into the input box.
Show a submission history so the owner can see everything they have ever fed to Sage, when it was analyzed, and what intelligence Sage extracted from it. This makes Sage a two way intelligence system where Sage finds intelligence on its own and the owner can also feed Sage anything they discover manually.
SAGE'S DEPARTMENT VIEW
Build Sage's department with five tabs.
Tab one is Industry Brief showing the current comprehensive industry intelligence profile organized into sections — Industry Landscape, Consumer Psychology, Marketing Best Practices, Seasonal Patterns, Regulatory Environment, Competitive Intelligence, and Emerging Opportunities. Each section shows Sage's latest findings in plain English with the date last updated and the sources Sage read. Show a Refresh button that triggers an immediate research cycle.
Tab two is Latest Intelligence showing a chronological feed of everything Sage has learned in the last 30 days. Each item shows the date, source type, a plain English summary, why it matters for the owner's business, and an Urgent badge on immediately significant items.
Tab three is Competitor Watch showing the confirmed competitor list for the selected brand. Each competitor shows their name, website, Facebook page, follower count, last post date, recent ad activity from the Facebook Ad Library, and a summary of what Sage has learned about their strategy. Show an Add Competitor button and a Remove button for each entry.
Tab four is Marketing Insights showing Sage's specific actionable recommendations based on its research. What headlines are working in this industry right now. What hooks are converting. What offers are resonating. What platforms are most effective. What seasonal opportunities are coming in the next 30 to 60 days. Each insight shows the source intelligence that led to the recommendation.
Tab five is Intelligence Input showing the drag and drop submission box and the full submission history.
SAGE IN THE MORNING BRIEFING
Echo includes a Sage Intelligence Update in every morning briefing referencing specific real things Sage actually found. When Sage finds urgent intelligence Echo delivers it immediately as a voice alert. Sage also contributes to the weekly strategic briefing with a monthly industry intelligence report summarizing the most important things learned, how that intelligence was applied, and predictions for the coming month.

This intelligence informs EchoAI's own product development and marketing strategy.
