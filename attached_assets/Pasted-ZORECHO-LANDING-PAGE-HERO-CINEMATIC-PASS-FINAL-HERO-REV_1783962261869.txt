1. Make the Zorecho Core feel alive

The Core is currently beautiful but static.

It must become the heartbeat of the page.

Idle Mode

The Core should continuously:

slowly breathe
softly illuminate
gently pulse
rotate the outer rings almost imperceptibly
move tiny particles around itself
animate the center waveform with a calm idle rhythm

Nothing should ever feel frozen.

Speaking Mode

When Echo begins speaking:

center waveform reacts naturally to the audio
cyan illumination increases
outer rings brighten
connection lines glow
tiny pulses travel outward toward the active department
surrounding light blooms increase smoothly

Never flash.

Never jitter.

Everything should feel expensive.
