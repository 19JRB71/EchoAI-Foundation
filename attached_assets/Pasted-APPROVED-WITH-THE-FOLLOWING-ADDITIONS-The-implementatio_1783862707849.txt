1. The Zorecho Core is the Heart of the Platform

The Core is not simply an animation.

It represents the intelligence of the entire AI company.

It should feel alive, calm, and premium.

Its movement should always communicate system state rather than exist as decoration.

Examples:

Idle → slow breathing
Listening → brighter outer ring
Thinking → internal particles and subtle ring rotation
Speaking → three illuminated bars animate with Echo's voice
Agent activity → colored pulse travels from the department to the Core
Critical issue → elegant red glow (never flashing)

The Core should become the visual identity of Zorecho.

2. Mission Control Should Feel Like Headquarters

Mission Control is no longer simply a dashboard.

It is Headquarters.

Everything on the page should support that feeling.

The first experience after login should answer one question:

"What did my AI company accomplish while I was away?"

Everything else comes second.

3. Do Not Invent Metrics

The concept artwork contains placeholder values.

Replace every placeholder with real platform data whenever possible.

If data does not exist:

omit it
or clearly label it as unavailable

Never fabricate business intelligence.

4. Live Agent Activity

Eventually I want every department to feel alive.

However...

If real-time status cannot be supported today...

Use honest status labels.

Examples:

Running

Waiting

Paused

Needs Connection

Disabled

Attention Required

Do not fake activity.

5. Motion Philosophy

Animation should feel like Apple.

Not like science fiction.

Nothing should spin continuously.

Nothing should flash.

Nothing should distract.

Movement should always communicate information.

The sidebar is not navigation.

Who they are
