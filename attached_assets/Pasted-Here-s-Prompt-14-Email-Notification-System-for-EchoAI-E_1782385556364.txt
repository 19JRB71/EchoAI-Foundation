Here's Prompt 14: Email Notification System for EchoAI:
EchoAI is an AI-powered SaaS marketing platform. You've built the complete system including authentication, billing, Facebook campaigns, brand discovery, lead qualification with voice, analytics, optimization, onboarding, and the admin panel. Now you need to build the email notification system that keeps customers informed automatically — weekly reports delivered to their inbox, payment reminders before their account gets locked, welcome emails after onboarding, and lead alerts when a hot lead comes in.
In the backend create an email utility file in the utils folder that initializes a transactional email service using Nodemailer with SMTP configuration from environment variables. Create reusable functions for sending single emails and bulk emails with proper error handling and retry logic.
In the controllers folder create an email controller with the following functions. First a send welcome email function that fires automatically after a customer completes onboarding, introduces them to EchoAI, tells them what to expect, and gives them a link back to their dashboard. Second a send weekly report email function that takes the AI generated weekly report from the analytics system and delivers it to the customer's email in a clean formatted template. Third a send hot lead alert function that fires automatically whenever the chatbot categorizes a lead as hot, notifies the business owner immediately with the lead's name, contact details, and a summary of what they said in the conversation. Fourth a send payment reminder email function that fires automatically three days before a subscription renewal and again on the day payment fails warning them their account will be locked in seven days. Fifth a send account locked email function that fires when an account gets locked telling them exactly how to update their payment method and restore access.
Create professional HTML email templates for each of the five email types. Keep them clean, simple, and on brand for EchoAI. Each template should have the EchoAI logo placeholder, clear subject line, friendly professional body, and a clear call to action button.
Wire the hot lead alert into the chatbot controller so it fires automatically whenever a lead is scored as hot. Wire the welcome email into the onboarding completion flow. Wire the payment emails into the Stripe webhook handler. Wire the weekly report email into the Monday morning scheduler.
In the routes folder create email routes with a POST route for manually triggering any email type for testing purposes. Protected with both auth and admin middleware so only you can trigger test emails.
Update server dot js to include email routes at slash api slash email. Add Nodemailer as a dependency. Output all files created.
