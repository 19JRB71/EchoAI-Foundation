Addition 2 — Executive Roundtable

Add a new architectural section titled:

Executive Roundtable

This is a structured collaboration pattern, not AI agents freely conversing.

When the owner asks a major strategic question (for example, "Should we increase Facebook ad spend?"), Echo may initiate a structured Executive Roundtable.

Echo requests input from only the departments relevant to the decision.

Example:

Sage → Strategic recommendation
Atlas → Advertising perspective
Pulse → Lead quality and sales impact
Scout → Competitive intelligence
Nova → Social implications
Forge → Creative considerations

Each department responds only from information it owns.

Echo never alters responses.

Echo simply summarizes:

Areas of agreement
Areas of disagreement
Supporting evidence
Risks
Final recommendation

The owner always makes the final decision.

This is not an AI discussion or consensus engine. It is a structured executive review conducted through the Collaboration Bus with all existing approval, ownership, evidence, and honesty rules preserved.

Addition 3 — Collaboration Philosophy

Add the following principle to the architecture:

Every department is responsible not only for performing its own responsibilities well, but also for improving the effectiveness of every other department whenever it possesses information those departments need.

This principle reinforces the purpose of the Collaboration Bus and establishes cooperation as a core architectural behavior rather than an optional enhancement.
