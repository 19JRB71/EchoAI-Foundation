Mission

We are redesigning Sage from the ground up to become the Chief Strategy Officer and Chief Marketing Intelligence Officer for every Zorecho customer.

This is not a feature update.

This is not a UI redesign.

This is a complete architectural redesign of Sage's intelligence, reasoning, learning, and strategic decision-making systems.

The objective is simple:

When Sage is complete, there should be no major strategic marketing responsibility that a world-class Chief Marketing Officer performs that Sage cannot either perform with evidence or clearly explain why additional information is required.

Why is it happening?
What does it mean for THIS business?
What should we do next?
