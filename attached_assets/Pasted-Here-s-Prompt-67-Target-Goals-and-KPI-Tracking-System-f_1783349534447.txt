Here's Prompt 67: Target Goals and KPI Tracking System for EchoAI:
EchoAI is an AI-powered SaaS marketing platform. You've built a complete autonomous system. Now you need to add a Target Goals and KPI Tracking System so every business has clear targets Echo works toward, measures against, and reports on every day. Echo should always know how close each business is to its goals and proactively adjust strategy to hit them.
PART 1 — GOAL SETTING SYSTEM
Build a goal setting interface in each brand's settings where the business owner can define their targets. Goals are organized into five categories.
Lead goals include monthly lead target which is the total number of new leads per month, cost per lead target which is the maximum acceptable cost per lead, and lead quality target which is the percentage of leads that should score as warm or hot.
Campaign goals include monthly ad spend limit which is the maximum total advertising budget per month, target return on ad spend which is the revenue generated per dollar spent on ads, and cost per click target which is the maximum acceptable cost per click.
Revenue goals include monthly revenue target which is the total revenue goal for the month, average deal value target which is the expected average value of each closed deal, and monthly conversion goal which is the number of leads that should convert to customers.
Content goals include weekly post frequency which is the number of social media posts per week per platform, engagement rate target which is the percentage of followers who should interact with each post, and follower growth target which is the number of new followers to gain per month.
Appointment goals include monthly appointment target which is the number of appointments to book per month, appointment show rate target which is the percentage of booked appointments that actually happen, and appointment to conversion rate which is the percentage of appointments that turn into sales.
Each goal has a target value, a current value updated daily, a percentage toward goal, a trend indicator showing whether it is improving or declining, and a projected end of month value based on current trajectory.
PART 2 — ECHO GOAL AWARENESS
Wire all goals into Echo's intelligence so Echo is always working toward the targets not just general optimization.
Every morning briefing Echo reports on goal progress across all active businesses. Echo says things like James you are at 23 leads this month against your goal of 50. You are on track to hit your goal if the current pace holds. Atlas has three campaign optimizations queued for today that should accelerate lead generation.
When a goal is at risk Echo alerts proactively. If the monthly lead goal is 50 and it is the 20th of the month with only 15 leads Echo says James I need to flag something. At the current pace Blacor Homes will finish the month with approximately 22 leads against your goal of 50. I have three recommendations to close the gap. Want to hear them?
When a goal is hit Echo celebrates genuinely and asks about next steps. James Blacor Homes just hit your monthly lead goal of 50 leads and there are still 8 days left in the month. That is ahead of schedule. Want me to increase the ad budget to maximize the remaining days or hold steady?
When performance is significantly exceeding goals Echo suggests scaling. James your cost per lead is $8.50 against your target of $15. You are getting leads at nearly half your target cost. This is a strong signal to increase ad spend. Want me to propose a budget increase for your approval?
Atlas uses the cost per lead target and return on ad spend target as optimization guardrails. Instead of optimizing for generic performance Atlas specifically optimizes to hit the defined targets. If the cost per lead target is $15 Atlas works to get cost per lead to $15 or below and stops cutting budget once that target is achieved.
PART 3 — GOAL DASHBOARD
Build a Goals and KPIs section inside each team member's department that shows that team member's relevant goals.
In Atlas's department show campaign goals with current versus target for ad spend, cost per lead, cost per click, and return on ad spend. Show a progress bar for each goal colored green if on track, yellow if slightly behind, and red if significantly off track.
In Nova's department show content goals with current versus target for post frequency, engagement rate, and follower growth per platform.
In Pulse's department show lead goals and appointment goals with current versus target for monthly leads, lead quality percentage, monthly appointments, show rate, and conversion rate.
In the ROI Dashboard show revenue goals with current versus target for monthly revenue, average deal value, and monthly conversions.
Build a unified Goals Overview accessible from Mission Control that shows every goal across every real business in one view. Show each business as a section with its goals listed. Show an overall goal achievement score for each business from zero to one hundred percent based on how many goals are currently on track.
PART 4 — GOAL BASED MORNING BRIEFING
Update the morning briefing to always include a goal progress summary. After covering overnight activity Echo summarizes goal status for each business in plain language. Something like Blacor Homes is running strong — leads are at 78 percent of monthly goal with 12 days remaining, cost per lead is under target at $11.20, and the content calendar is fully scheduled. The affiliate site needs attention — click through rate is at 4 percent against your 10 percent target. I have two recommendations to improve it. Want to hear them now or after the full briefing?
PART 5 — GOAL ALERTS AND NOTIFICATIONS
Build a goal alert system that monitors all goals daily and sends voice alerts and push notifications when important thresholds are crossed.
Alert when any goal reaches 50 percent of the month with less than 30 percent progress — early warning that intervention is needed.
Alert when any goal reaches 75 percent of the month with less than 60 percent progress — urgent warning that significant action is needed.
Alert when any goal is achieved — celebration and prompt to consider scaling.
Alert when any metric improves by more than 20 percent in a single day — positive signal worth knowing about.
Alert when any metric declines by more than 20 percent in a single day — negative signal requiring investigation.
All alerts go through Echo's voice system and are also logged in the Mission Control attention panel.
PART 6 — AFFILIATE SPECIFIC GOALS
Since the owner is testing an affiliate marketing business build specific goal types for affiliate businesses. Affiliate goals include monthly click target which is total clicks sent to the affiliate site, landing page conversion rate target which is percentage of ad clicks that opt in on the landing page, click through rate target which is percentage of landing page visitors who click through to the affiliate site, monthly commission goal which is the total commission expected per month, and cost per acquisition target which is the maximum acceptable ad spend per confirmed affiliate signup.
Wire affiliate goals into Atlas's optimization so Atlas specifically optimizes for click through rate and cost per acquisition when the brand is configured as an affiliate business.
Add a brand type selector in brand settings with options for standard business, affiliate marketing, e-commerce, service business, and restaurant. Each brand type shows the relevant goal categories and hides irrelevant ones. An affiliate business does not need appointment goals. A service business does not need e-commerce goals.
Build a goal setup wizard that runs automatically after the Setup Agent completes for a new brand. Echo asks the business owner what their goals are conversationally. How many leads do you want per month? What is your budget? What does success look like in 90 days? Echo converts the answers into specific measurable targets and saves them automatically.
Wire all goal tracking into the existing Monday morning scheduler so goal progress is calculated and updated automatically every day. Wire goal alerts into the existing voice alert and push notification systems. Wire goal awareness into every Echo briefing, every Atlas optimization, and every Sentinel health check. Output all files created and updated.
