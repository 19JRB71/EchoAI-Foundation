Sage Enhancement: Pattern Intelligence Engine (PIE)

We are adding a new core capability to Sage called the Pattern Intelligence Engine.

The purpose of this engine is NOT to copy competitors.

Its purpose is to discover patterns in successful public marketing campaigns, understand why they are effective, and provide strategic guidance that helps Forge create original, higher-performing marketing.

Mission

Sage must continuously study publicly available marketing within each client's industry and answer one question:

"What patterns consistently make people stop, engage, and convert?"

The goal is to understand why campaigns work—not duplicate them.

Intelligence Report

Instead of saying:

"Competitor ABC used this ad."

Sage should say:

"Across 1,200 publicly available campaigns in this industry, educational videos with customer testimonials and bright natural lighting consistently receive higher engagement than promotional graphics."

Focus on industry-wide insights, not individual competitors.

Collaboration with Forge

After analysis, Sage automatically generates a Creative Brief for Forge.

Example:

Creative Brief

Objective:
Increase engagement.

Recommended Hook:
Customer Problem.

Recommended Emotion:
Trust.

Recommended Visual Style:
Cinematic documentary.

Recommended Camera:
Drone opening followed by close-up craftsmanship.

Recommended CTA:
Soft educational invitation.

Recommended Color Palette:
Warm natural tones.

Recommended Story:
Show transformation instead of simply showing the finished product.

Forge must create completely original marketing inspired by these insights.

Never imitate another company's branding, copy, imagery, or distinctive creative assets.

Core Principle

Sage is not a competitor monitoring tool.

Sage is an AI Marketing Intelligence Analyst.

Its responsibility is to understand why marketing works and provide strategic intelligence that enables Forge to create original, higher-performing campaigns.

Every recommendation should answer one question:

"What can we learn from successful public marketing that helps our client create something even better?"
