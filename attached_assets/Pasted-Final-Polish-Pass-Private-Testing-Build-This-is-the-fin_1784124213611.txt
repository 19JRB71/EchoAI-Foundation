Final Polish Pass (Private Testing Build)

This is the final polish pass before we begin private testing.

Do NOT redesign the onboarding flow.
Do NOT move sections or change the layout.
The structure is approved.

Only make the refinements below.

1. Completion Screen

Keep the layout exactly as it is.

Replace the text with something that feels more emotional and memorable.

Suggested copy:

Congratulations, Sir.

Your AI company is now online.

Echo, Scout, Atlas, Nova, Pulse, Voice, Forge, Sentinel, and Sage are standing by.

While you focus on running your business,
we'll focus on growing it.

Welcome to Zorecho.

Welcome to your AI company.

After Echo finishes speaking, ask:

Would you like a two-minute guided tour of your headquarters?

2. Mission Control

Keep the existing checklist.

Rename:

Company Setup

to

AI Company Activation

Instead of showing 4/8, display a progress bar with a completion percentage.

Example:

AI Company Activation
50% Complete

People naturally understand percentages better than fractions.

3. Connection Cards

Continue focusing on benefits instead of features.

Each connection should explain why it matters.

Examples:

Facebook

Connect Facebook so Nova can publish posts, create ads, and manage your social media automatically.

Google

Connect Google so Echo can monitor Gmail, manage your calendar, schedule appointments, and organize important emails.

Every integration should answer one question:

"Why should I connect this?"

4. Echo's Personality

Without changing functionality, make Echo feel warmer and more human.

Examples:

Instead of:

Facebook connected.

Use:

Excellent, Sir. Facebook is connected. Nova now has everything she needs to begin working for you.

Instead of:

Google connected.

Use:

Beautiful. Google is online. Your calendar, Gmail, and scheduling tools are now available to your AI team.

Echo should feel like an executive assistant—not system software.

5. Celebration

When onboarding reaches 100%, Mission Control should feel like it is powering on.

Subtle animations only:

Zorecho Core illuminates.
Connection lines animate.
Small glow behind the Core.
Dashboard fades in smoothly.

No flashy effects.

Think NASA Mission Control, not a video game.

6. No Other Changes

No layout changes.

No feature changes.

No redesign.

No additional screens.

After these refinements are complete, deploy this build to the private testing environment only.
