Here's Prompt 50: Interactive Guided Tour for EchoAI — The Final Prompt:
EchoAI is an AI-powered SaaS marketing platform. You've built a complete 49-prompt production-ready system. Now you need to add the final touch — an Interactive Guided Tour that walks new users through the platform step by step, highlights every feature, shows them exactly what to do first, and makes them feel confident and excited from their very first login.
The guided tour must be tier-aware. Starter customers see a tour of their Starter features only. Professional customers see a tour of all Professional features. Enterprise customers see the full tour of everything. Admin accounts see a special admin tour covering the admin panel, platform management, and how to manage customers.
In the client source folder create a complete guided tour system. Build a TourEngine component that manages the entire tour experience. The tour engine overlays the live dashboard with a semi-transparent backdrop, highlights the current element with a bright spotlight effect, shows a tooltip card next to the highlighted element with a step title, a two to three sentence explanation of what the feature does and why it matters, and navigation buttons for Previous, Next, and Skip Tour. Show a progress bar at the top of the screen showing which step the user is on out of the total steps. Show a step counter such as Step 3 of 12 in the tour card. The spotlight should smoothly animate between elements as the user progresses through steps. The backdrop should dim everything except the highlighted element. The tour card should intelligently position itself to avoid going off screen.
Create four separate tour sequences. First the Starter Tour covering twelve steps. Step one highlights the Dashboard and explains the three key metrics. Step two highlights the Brand selector and explains how to switch between businesses. Step three highlights the Leads section and explains the CRM and temperature categories. Step four highlights the Campaigns section and explains Facebook ad automation. Step five highlights the Website Chatbot section and explains the embeddable widget. Step six highlights the Email Marketing section and explains campaigns and drip sequences. Step seven highlights the Social Media section and explains the two platform posting limit. Step eight highlights the Settings section and explains account configuration. Step nine highlights the team member invite button and explains collaboration. Step ten highlights the weekly report feature and explains automatic delivery. Step eleven highlights the upgrade prompt on a locked Professional feature and explains what unlocks at Professional tier. Step twelve is a completion celebration screen with confetti animation congratulating them and telling them their first campaign will be built within twenty four hours.
Second the Professional Tour covering eighteen steps that includes all Starter steps plus step thirteen highlights the Voice Chatbot and explains voice lead qualification. Step fourteen highlights the Phone Agent and explains outbound AI calling. Step fifteen highlights the Ad Creative Studio and explains five-concept creative generation. Step sixteen highlights the Content Calendar and explains thirty day automated posting. Step seventeen highlights the Follow-Up Sequences and explains the seven touchpoint automation. Step eighteen is a completion celebration with confetti and a message about their Customer Intelligence Engine warming up.
Third the Enterprise Tour covering twenty four steps that includes all Professional steps plus step nineteen highlights the ROI Dashboard and explains revenue attribution. Step twenty highlights the Customer Intelligence Engine and explains the weekly strategic brief. Step twenty one highlights the White Label section and explains agency reselling. Step twenty two highlights the Affiliate Program and explains commission earning. Step twenty three highlights the Feedback section and explains automated sentiment analysis. Step twenty four is a grand completion celebration with confetti, a personalized message using the business owner's name and business name, and a button to book their personal onboarding call with you.
Fourth the Admin Tour covering ten steps walking through the admin panel, customer management, platform stats, agency management, affiliate management, and how to lock and unlock accounts.
Build a tour trigger system. The tour launches automatically for new users who just completed onboarding. Show a floating help button in the bottom right corner of every dashboard page that says Take the Tour. This button is always visible so users can restart the tour anytime. Store tour completion status in the database so it persists across devices. Add a Tour and Help section to the Settings panel showing tour completion status and a Restart Tour button.
Build a contextual help system alongside the tour. Every section in the dashboard gets a small question mark icon in the header. Clicking it shows a mini help card explaining what that section does, its key features, and a tip for getting the most out of it. These help cards are always available independently of the tour.
Add a Welcome Modal that appears the very first time a new user logs in after completing onboarding. The modal shows a warm personalized welcome using their business name, a three point summary of what EchoAI will do for them this week, and two buttons — Start the Tour and Go to Dashboard.
In the backend add a tour progress endpoint. Create a tour controller with a save tour progress function that saves the user's current tour step and completion status, a get tour status function that returns whether the tour has been completed and the last step reached, and a complete tour function that marks the tour as done and records the completion date.
Create a migration file that adds a tour progress table with columns for progress ID, user ID, tour type, current step, completed boolean, completed at, and timestamps.
Make the tour visually stunning. Use smooth CSS transitions for spotlight movement. Use a pulsing ring animation on highlighted elements. Use slide-in animations for the tour card. Use a spring animation for the progress bar. The completion screen should have a full confetti explosion using a lightweight confetti library. The overall tour should feel like a premium product experience that makes users genuinely excited about what they just signed up for.
Output all files created and updated. This is the final prompt — make it exceptional.
