Here's Prompt 16: Mobile Responsive Voice Landing Page and Lead Capture for EchoAI:
EchoAI is an AI-powered SaaS marketing platform. You've built the complete system including the public landing page. Now you need to build a separate standalone mobile-optimized voice lead capture page that your customers can use as their Facebook ad landing page. This is the page prospects land on after clicking a Facebook ad. It needs to load fast, look great on mobile, and immediately engage them in a voice conversation with the AI qualification chatbot.
In the client source folder create a standalone voice landing page that is completely separate from the main EchoAI dashboard and public landing page. This page is accessed via a unique URL that includes the customer's brand ID so the chatbot knows which business it represents.
The page should have four sections.
Section one is the business header. Dynamically load the business name and a brief tagline from the brand profile using the brand ID in the URL. Show a simple clean header with the business name. No EchoAI branding on this page since it represents the customer's business not EchoAI itself.
Section two is the voice chatbot interface. Large centered microphone button that pulses gently to invite interaction. When the page loads the AI chatbot automatically introduces itself in the business owner's brand voice and asks the first qualifying question. The prospect taps the microphone to respond. The conversation continues naturally back and forth. Show a live transcript below so prospects can read along. Keep the interface clean and distraction free.
Section three is the contact capture. After two or three exchanges the chatbot naturally asks for the prospect's name, phone number, and email so the business owner can follow up. Once captured these are saved to the leads table linked to the correct brand and the lead temperature scoring continues based on the conversation.
Section four is the closing. Once the conversation reaches a natural end the chatbot thanks them, tells them someone will be in touch shortly, and shows a simple confirmation message. If the lead is hot it fires the instant notification email to the business owner immediately.
Make this page extremely fast loading since it is a Facebook ad landing page and load speed affects ad quality scores. Mobile first design. Large touch targets. Clean bold typography. The background and accent colors should eventually be customizable per brand but for now use a clean professional default.
Create a public backend endpoint that accepts the brand ID and returns the brand's public profile — just the business name, tagline, and voice description — without exposing any sensitive data.
Add a new route in Express at slash api slash public slash brand slash brand ID for this public brand info endpoint. No authentication required since prospects are not logged in.
Update the React router to add slash voice slash brand ID as a new public route that renders the voice landing page.
Output all files created.
