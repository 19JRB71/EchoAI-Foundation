ZORECHO MISSION CONTROL – OFFICIAL DESIGN SPECIFICATION (V1)

The attached Mission Control concept image is now the official visual direction for Zorecho.

This concept supersedes all previous Mission Control concepts.

Your responsibility is to faithfully implement this design while preserving the existing platform architecture and functionality.

Do not redesign the interface.

Do not reinterpret the layout.

Do not simplify the experience.

If something cannot be implemented exactly, explain why and provide options before making changes.

OBJECTIVE

Mission Control is not a dashboard.

Mission Control is the Headquarters of the customer's AI company.

When customers log in they should immediately feel like they have stepped into the command center of a company that has been working for them around the clock.

The very first question Mission Control should answer is:

"What did my AI company accomplish while I was away?"

Everything else is secondary.

THE ZORECHO CORE

The Zorecho Core represents the central intelligence of the entire AI company.

It is not a person.

It is the brain that connects every AI department.

The Core should always feel alive.

It should never appear static.

Core States

Idle

Slow breathing glow
Gentle blue pulse

Listening

Outer ring brightens
Internal particles become calm
Indicates the system is listening

Thinking

Internal particles orbit
Waveform slows
Outer ring rotates subtly

Speaking

The Core illuminates
Waveform animates naturally
Outer ring pulses with the conversation

Agent Activity

Colored pulses travel from departments into the Core
The Core briefly acknowledges incoming information

Critical

Calm red glow
Never flashing
Never distracting

All animations should feel premium and purposeful.

ECHO

Echo remains the customer's Executive Assistant.

Echo is the personality customers interact with.

Echo:

Delivers the Morning Briefing
Answers questions
Coordinates departments
Explains business activity
Speaks on behalf of the Zorecho Core

The relationship is:

The Core thinks.

Echo communicates.

When Echo is active:

Echo's sidebar icon becomes active.
The Core enters Listening, Thinking, or Speaking mode.
Both should work together visually.
QUICK SPEAK

Remove the dedicated Echo Voice panel.

Instead...

Place one premium Talk to Echo button directly beneath the Zorecho Core.

When pressed:

Voice mode begins.
Echo becomes active.
The Core animates accordingly.

This becomes the primary voice interaction for the platform.
