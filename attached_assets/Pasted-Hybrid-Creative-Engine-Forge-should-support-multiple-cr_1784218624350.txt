Hybrid Creative Engine

Forge should support multiple creative workflows rather than assuming every customer wants AI-generated content or only customer media.

Before every campaign, determine the best creation method based on available assets, customer preferences, campaign goals, and subscription level.

Creative Modes
Mode 1 — Brand Asset Mode (Preferred)

Use customer-owned photos and videos.

Enhance, clean, edit, recompose, relight, and optimize them for marketing.

Ideal for:

Pole barns
Roofing
HVAC
Construction
Landscaping
Auto dealerships
Real estate
Manufacturing
Mode 2 — AI Assisted Mode

Combine customer assets with AI.

Examples:

Replace the sky
Change the season
Improve landscaping
Add lighting
Remove distractions
Add tasteful environmental elements
Create new marketing compositions

The customer's actual product remains the centerpiece.

Mode 3 — AI Creative Mode

When no suitable assets exist, Forge creates original marketing using AI.

Examples:

Brand awareness campaigns
Holiday promotions
Educational graphics
Industry tips
Concept art
Lifestyle imagery
Team recruitment
Service illustrations
Community engagement posts

These campaigns should reinforce the customer's brand without pretending to depict a specific real product unless based on customer-authorized assets.

Mode 4 — Hybrid Campaign Mode (Recommended)

Mix all three approaches.

Example weekly schedule:

Monday
Customer photo enhanced.

Tuesday
AI educational infographic.

Wednesday
Customer video edited.

Thursday
AI lifestyle image.

Friday
Customer testimonial.

Saturday
AI seasonal campaign.

Sunday
Behind-the-scenes photo.

Feeds stay fresh while reducing costs.

Campaign Creation Decision Tree

Before creating content, Forge asks:

Does the customer have suitable media?

If YES:

Use Asset Mode.

Would AI enhancement improve the result?

If YES:

Use AI Assisted Mode.

Does this campaign benefit from an original AI concept?

If YES:

Use AI Creative Mode.

Would combining approaches create a stronger campaign?

If YES:

Use Hybrid Campaign Mode.

Industry Intelligence

Different industries should automatically favor different modes.

Example:

Realtor

70% customer media

30% AI generated

Dealership

80% inventory photos

20% AI branding

Pole Barn Builder

60% customer installs

40% AI educational and lifestyle content

Restaurant

70% real food

30% AI promotions

Law Firm

Mostly original photography with AI-created educational graphics and branded visuals.

Echo Interaction

Echo can occasionally ask:

"I noticed we're running low on fresh project photos. Uploading 10 new images will improve your next month's marketing."

Or:

"Would you like this week's campaign to focus more on your real projects, or would you like me to create a few original AI concepts to add variety?"

Core Principle

The customer should never have to think about how the content is created.

They should simply feel that every campaign is fresh, authentic, and tailored to their business.

One more idea

I actually wouldn't make this a one-time setting buried in a menu.

I'd let Echo ask naturally when it makes sense:

"For this campaign, would you like me to showcase one of your actual projects, create something entirely new, or surprise you with what I think will perform best?"
