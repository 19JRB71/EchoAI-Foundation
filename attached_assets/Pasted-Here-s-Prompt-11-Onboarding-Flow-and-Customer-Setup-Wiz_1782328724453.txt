Here's Prompt 11: Onboarding Flow and Customer Setup Wizard for EchoAI:
EchoAI is an AI-powered SaaS marketing platform. You've built the full backend and frontend dashboard. Now you need to build the customer onboarding flow — the guided setup wizard that walks a new customer through everything they need to get started after their sales call with you. This is the first thing they see after registering and it needs to feel smooth, professional, and confidence-building.
In the client source folder create an onboarding wizard component that guides new users through five steps in sequence.
Step one is welcome and account setup. Show a warm welcome message explaining what EchoAI is and what it will do for them. Ask them to confirm their business name, their industry or niche, and how many team members will be using the platform. Save this to their user profile.
Step two is Facebook account connection. Walk them through connecting their Facebook ad account step by step. Show clear instructions, a connect button that triggers the Facebook OAuth flow, and a confirmation message when successfully connected. If they skip this step allow them to complete it later from settings.
Step three is subscription selection. Show the three pricing tiers clearly — starter for one user, professional for up to five users, enterprise for unlimited users — each at fifty dollars per seat per month. Allow them to select their tier and enter payment details powered by Stripe. Show exactly what they get with each tier.
Step four is brand discovery introduction. Explain that EchoAI is about to get to know their brand deeply so it can represent them authentically. Tell them this is a three part conversation and they should answer naturally and honestly. Then launch directly into the brand discovery conversation they will have with the AI agent.
Step five is confirmation and launch. Show a summary of everything set up — Facebook connected, subscription active, brand profile complete. Show a countdown or progress indicator and then launch them into the main dashboard with a congratulations message. Tell them their first campaign will be built within twenty four hours.
In the client source folder also create an onboarding progress tracker that saves which steps are completed to the backend so if they close the browser and come back they pick up exactly where they left off. Store onboarding status in the users table by adding an onboarding completed boolean and onboarding step integer column via a new migration.
Wire the onboarding wizard to show automatically for any new user who has not completed onboarding. Once onboarding is complete redirect them to the main dashboard and never show the wizard again.
Output all files created.
