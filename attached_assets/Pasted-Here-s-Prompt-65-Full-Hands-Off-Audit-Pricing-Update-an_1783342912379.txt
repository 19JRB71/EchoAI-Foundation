Weekly reports — Echo must automatically generate and deliver the weekly report every Monday morning without any user trigger. Verify the scheduler is running this and fix any gaps.
Competitor intelligence — Scout must automatically scan for competitor activity every 6 hours and prepare a briefing without any user trigger. Verify this is running and fix any gaps.

For every gap found fix it immediately and document what was broken and what was fixed.
